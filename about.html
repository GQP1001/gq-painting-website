About Us

Greene Quality Painting LLC was created to elevate homes and spaces in a clean, fun, and astonishing way. We pride ourselves on both our expertise and exceptional customer service. At Greene Quality Painting, we strive to set ourselves apart from other contractors by making customers feel special. We understand that this is a service to you, which is why we're committed to excellent communication and support from the estimating process through well after the project is complete. Greene Quality Painting looks forward to helping you with your next project.

Meet Ethan

Ethan Greene is the owner of Greene Quality Painting with over 5 years of painting industry experience and additional background in construction. He moved to Clarksville, Tennessee to build this business and provide the quality results and exceptional customer service that homeowners deserve. Ethan has participated in architecture programs at Salt Lake Community College and Boston Architectural College, giving him the eye for design and attention to detail that sets our work apart.
